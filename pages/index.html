What is Calico Recovery?

It is a recovery app I am starting as a person in the middle of my own recovery journey.

Features

  • 1 on 1 therapy/recovery with sponsors or employees
  • Group therapy and recovery
  • Family-to-family support
  • In-app gambling cessation
  • Cessation tools for all kinds
  • No involuntary admissions
  • Mining-pool-based donation system
  • Offline XMR wallet for operations
  • Gold buying/selling interface
  • Employee feature unlocks
  • Monthly withdrawal rules
  • ROI for keeping funds in the bank
